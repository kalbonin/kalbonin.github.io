Why are we tackling this problem?

There are three reasons that we think this is an important problem to create a solution for. In the last several years, the number of cycling computers has increased dramatically. Out of the five members of our group, four members have been in a cycling accident. The most detrimental and common of all cycling accidents are the ones involving motor vehicles.

So build away, we do.
